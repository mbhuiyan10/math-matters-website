About Math Matters

Affordable tutoring, invaluable learning — serving Queens families for over a decade.

Our Story

Math Matters Tutoring Center opened its doors in 2010 on 37th Avenue and 82nd Street in Elmhurst, Queens. What began as a single classroom grew into a trusted community resource; at its peak serving over 200 students across three locations in Queens, including near the 74th Street subway station and Junction Boulevard in Corona.

From the start, Math Matters was built on a simple principle: quality tutoring should be accessible to every family. No contracts, no large upfront packages, just affordable, results-driven instruction in small classes of 5–6 students, where every child gets the attention they deserve.

Today, Math Matters is entering a new chapter. With fresh energy, updated programs, and a renewed commitment to the community, we continue to help students across Queens & NYC prepare for the SAT, SHSAT, NYS Regents, and NYS State Tests — one student at a time.

Meet the Founder

Syed Hossain grew up in Bangladesh and was educated across Europe before spending three and a half years as a scientist at Procter & Gamble in Newcastle, England. After joining his family in Queens, he served as an adjunct professor at LaGuardia Community College — and it was there that his passion for teaching young people took root.

In 2010, Syed founded Math Matters with a mission to fill the gaps the school system couldn't. He assembled a team of retired professors and graduate students, designed personalized lesson plans built around each student's entrance exams and report cards, and kept class sizes small so no child would be overlooked.

“The things I’ve learned so far, I’d like to transfer to young people. This is the small journey that I’ve started.”

— Syed Hossain, Founder

Our Approach

How we help students achieve real, lasting results

Small Groups

We keep our class sizes small so every student gets the individual attention they need. No one falls through the cracks at Math Matters.

We believe students learn by doing. Our programs are built around extensive practice with real exam questions and hands-on problem solving.
