SAT Prep

Strategic preparation for college-bound students

The SAT is one of the most important tests in your child's academic journey. Our SAT prep program is designed to build the skills, strategies, and confidence students need to achieve their target scores.

  • Comprehensive review of Math and Evidence-Based Reading & Writing sections
  • Full-length practice tests under timed, realistic conditions
  • Targeted skill building in areas where your child needs the most improvement
  • Proven test-taking strategies and time management techniques
  • Regular progress tracking and score analysis

Who it's for: High school students (typically grades 10-12) preparing for college admissions.

SHSAT Prep

Your child's path to NYC's top specialized high schools

Admission to NYC's Specialized High Schools — Stuyvesant, Bronx Science, Brooklyn Tech, and more — is determined by a single test: the SHSAT. Our intensive prep program gives students the edge they need to earn their spot.

  • In-depth coverage of the ELA and Math sections of the SHSAT
  • Hundreds of practice problems modeled after real SHSAT questions
  • Strategies for the most challenging problem types
  • Timed practice tests to build speed and accuracy
  • Personalized feedback and study plans

Who it's for: Students in grades 8-9 applying to NYC Specialized High Schools.

NYS Regents Tutoring

Pass your Regents exams with confidence

New York State Regents exams are required for high school graduation and can impact your child's diploma type. Our tutoring focuses on the specific content and skills tested on each Regents exam.

  • Subject-specific preparation covering key Regents topics
  • Review of past Regents exams and commonly tested concepts
  • Step-by-step problem-solving techniques
  • Focus on both multiple choice and constructed response questions
  • Study plans tailored to each student's exam schedule

Who it's for: High school students (grades 9-12) preparing for NYS Regents examinations.
